Multi-input, multi-output

Multi-force input → multi-response output.

The surrogate is MIMO: forces at every floor map to displacements at every floor, matched against FEM.

This slide makes the MIMO nature explicit. TOP panel: the applied wind forces — spatially non-uniform across floors (each floor sees a different force history). BOTTOM panel: the resulting per-floor displacement responses, FEM (solid blue) vs the surrogate (dashed orange) — the model tracks every floor's response, not just one. Switch load case with the buttons (wind / constant / sine): constant/sine apply a spatially uniform force at every floor; wind is genuinely non-uniform. The time sweep autoplays on entry. One forward pass produces the full multi-floor response field.
