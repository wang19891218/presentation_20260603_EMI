The gap

Conventional surrogates don't transfer across buildings.

Different height, stiffness, damping, mass — one surrogate per building doesn't transfer.

Key point: cross-building generalization is the fundamental bottleneck. Transfer learning still requires target data and degrades when structural properties differ significantly (Pan 2020, Tan 2018). The SVG (slide03_one_vs_many.svg) shows the contrast: many per-building models on the left vs one shared HyperNetwork on the right. Walk through the left side first (many N₁…Nₙ boxes, retrain each time), then pivot right (single HyperNet box, adapts from features).
