The model was trained and tested on four load classes. Wind: excellent at 1.25% population mean NRMSE on 900 held-out buildings. Constant and near-resonance sine: strong results (4.6% and 13.7%). Impulse at 54.2% is an honest current limitation — it reflects the difficulty of short-duration impulsive loading for an autoregressive time-domain LSTM rollout, and it is shown in red rather than buried. All numbers are population means over 900 held-out test buildings, fully disjoint from the 7,200 training buildings. The bar chart stages one load class at a time per fragment click (wind, constant, sine, impulse). Extending to richer and more complex load types — including improving impulse coverage — is active future work covered on the next slide.
