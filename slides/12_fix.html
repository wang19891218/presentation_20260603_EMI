Constant (4.6 %) and sine (13.7 %) confirm the surrogate generalizes beyond wind. Click to reveal each load class.

The model was trained primarily on wind but tested on four load classes. Wind: excellent at 1.25% population mean NRMSE on 900 held-out buildings. Constant and near-resonance sine: strong results (4.6% and 13.7%), demonstrating genuine generalization beyond the training distribution. All numbers are population means over 900 held-out test buildings, fully disjoint from the 7,200 training buildings. The bar chart stages one load class at a time per fragment click (wind, constant, sine). Extending to richer and more complex load types is active future work covered on the next slide.
